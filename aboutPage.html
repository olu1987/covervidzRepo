About Us

Covervidz is a web application that allows anyone to search for, view, upload and share artistic cover videos that have been posted to youtube. Covervidz has an already extensive library of some of the best and most popular covers and we are always adding. We also allow YOU to add to our library!. The only requirements are that the video must be on youtube first before it is added to Covervidz, and also that the video is actually a cover of an original song that has been released. This site is for covers only, not original songs, and not non-music related content

For enquiries about covervidz feel free to contact us on our facebook page www.facebook.com/covervidz or email covervidz@gmail.com
